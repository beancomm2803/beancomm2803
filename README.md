<h3>Welcome to the official GitHub of BEAN (Bhartiya Engineering Achievers Network).</h3>

<p><strong>BEAN</strong> was founded with a simple belief:
  <strong>Engineering is not learned through endless consumption. It is learned through building.</strong>
  <p>We are a community of students, builders, founders, thinkers, and aspiring engineers who want to transform ideas into projects, projects into products, and products into opportunities.
    ---
    <strong>🚀 What We Do: </strong>
    At BEAN, we focus on:
    <ul>
      <li> Building real-world projects</li>
      <li> Learning by doing</li>
      <li> Accountability and consistency</li>
      <li> Technical skill development</li>
      <li> Startup and product thinking</li>
      <li> Community-driven growth</li>
    </ul>

Through workshops, build sprints, competitions, showcases, and collaborative projects, members are encouraged to move beyond tutorials and create tangible outcomes.

---

<strong> 🏗 BEAN Summer Build Sprint 2026 </strong>

The Summer Build Sprint is our flagship community initiative.

Over 8 weeks, participants:

<ul>
  <li> Learn modern technologies</li>
  <li> Build projects</li>
  <li> Deploy their work</li>
  <li> Maintain GitHub activity</li>
  <li> Showcase progress publicly</li>
</ul>
The objective is simple:

<strong><bold>Build. Ship. Showcase.</bold></strong>
</p>
</p>
